Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games developed and published by Halfroom Studio ("we", "our", or "us") on the Android platform. By installing or playing any of our games, you agree to this policy and the collection and use of information as described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly. However, our games integrate third-party services that may collect and process certain data to provide their functionalities.

Third-Party Services

Unity Ads

Unity Ads is used to serve in-game advertisements. It may collect and use data such as:


  • - Device Information: device type, operating system, and other technical details
  • - Advertising Identifiers: such as the Android Advertising ID
  • - IP Address: used to infer approximate location
  • - App Usage Data: interactions with ads and the app

This data is used to:


  • - Serve relevant advertisements
  • - Limit the number of times the same ad is shown
  • - Detect and prevent fraud
  • - Measure ad performance

More information: Unity Privacy Policy

Unity Analytics

Unity Analytics helps us understand how players interact with our games. It may collect:


  • - Device Information: device type, OS, and technical specs
  • - IP Address: used to infer approximate location
  • - App Usage Data: levels completed, session length, gameplay behavior
  • - Crash Reports: info about app crashes or bugs

This data helps us:


  • - Improve game performance and stability
  • - Understand gameplay patterns
  • - Detect bugs and optimize user experience

More information: Unity Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

Google AdMob is used to display banner, interstitial, or rewarded ads. It may collect:


  • - Device Information: device type, OS, and specifications
  • - Advertising Identifiers: such as the Android Advertising ID
  • - IP Address: used to infer approximate location
  • - App Usage Data: ad views, clicks, and interactions

This data is used to:


  • - Serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • - Measure ad effectiveness
  • - Detect and prevent ad fraud

More information: Google Privacy & Terms

Use of Approximate Location

Unity Ads, Unity Analytics, and Google AdMob may use your IP address to infer your approximate location (such as country or city). This data is not collected by Halfroom Studio, but is used by the services to:


  • - Serve region-appropriate advertisements
  • - Comply with regional legal requirements
  • - Analyze usage patterns by region

We do not store or process your location information ourselves. All such data handling is managed by the third-party services listed above according to their own privacy practices.

2. How We Use the Data

The information collected by third-party services is used for:

  • - Showing ads and tracking ad performance
  • - Understanding game usage and improving gameplay experience
  • - Crash reports and technical issue diagnostics

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell or share your personal data. Data collected through third-party services is stored and processed by those providers according to their respective privacy policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it as soon as possible.

5. Opt-Out and Control

Users may limit tracking by adjusting their device’s privacy settings, such as disabling ad personalization in Android settings. You can also review and opt-out of data collection via each third-party provider’s policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Such changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date above.
