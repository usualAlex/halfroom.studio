About Us

Halfroom Studio is a very small indie dev studio based in Romania. As small as half of a room and mainly, a one-person studio. We love creating compact but action-packed games with unique controls, awesome physics, puzzles and addictive gameplay. We aim to create fun games that can be played on a whip, on the go. Just simple mindless fun.

Halfroom Studio was founded as a way to experiment with game development and create old school fun driven games.
